Bonjour · Welcome

Our story

A neighbourhood Persian market, proudly part of Montréal — where the flavours of Iran meet the corner of Sherbrooke and home.

Since our grand opening

A taste of home in NDG

Marché Shahran opened its doors at 6010 Sherbrooke Street West to bring the Iranian grocery experience to Notre-Dame-de-Grâce — the aisles, the aromas and the warm welcome you remember from back home.

At around 6,000 square feet, we’re smaller than the big stores — and that’s the point. Everything is close at hand, the team knows the products, and there’s always someone at the counter to help you choose. Whether you grew up with this food or you’re discovering it for the first time, there’s a place for you here.

What we stand for

Small store, big care

Always Halal

Every cut of meat and chicken is certified Halal and prepared in-house by our butchers.

Fresh first

Produce, herbs and bread restocked daily, and an open counter you can taste before you buy.

Family-run

Independent and locally owned — real people who remember your name and your order.

Rooted in the community

Iranian at heart, Montréalais in spirit — a market that belongs to the neighbourhood.

“A market should feel like someone’s kitchen — generous, fresh, and full of stories.”

From the saffron in the spice aisle to the kabab on the grill, every corner of Marché Shahran is here to make your table feel a little more like home.

Come say hello

Find us on Sherbrooke West

Look for the red awning at 6010 Sherbrooke Street West, in the heart of NDG. We’re open every day — drop in, taste something new, and let us help you find a piece of home.
