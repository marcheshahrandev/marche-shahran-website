What we carry

Our aisles

A full Persian pantry in 6,000 sq ft — fresh, frozen, Halal and hand-served. Everything is available in-store; browse the categories below and discover what's on our shelves.

Fresh daily

Fruit & Vegetables

A long refrigerated wall of fruit and vegetables, restocked every morning — including the Persian herbs and specialties that are hard to find anywhere else in the city.

  • Fresh sabzi — parsley, cilantro, dill, tarragon, fenugreek
  • Persian cucumbers, eggplant, herbs & seasonal fruit
  • Pomegranate, quince, sour cherries & dates in season
✓ 100% Halal

Meat & Chicken

Our full-service butcher cuts fresh beef, lamb and chicken every day — all certified Halal. Ask for it whole, portioned, ground or trimmed exactly how your recipe needs it.

  • Beef, veal & lamb — shank, shoulder, ground & more
  • Whole & portioned chicken, plus offal on request
  • Custom and bulk cuts for parties & gatherings
✓ Halal

Marinated Meat & Chicken

House-marinated and ready for the grill. Pick it up on your way to a barbecue and skip the prep — the saffron and onion have already done their work.

  • Koobideh, barg & chenjeh beef kabab
  • Joojeh — saffron & plain chicken kabab
  • Lamb chops and seasonal marinades
Served by hand

Olives, Cheese & Nuts

Our open counter is the heart of the store. Taste, choose and take home exactly the amount you want — an attendant scoops it fresh for you.

  • Kalamata, Lebanese green, Moroccan black, stuffed & marinated olives
  • Feta & white cheeses — Bulgarian, Iranian & Greek-style
  • Roasted nuts, ajil mixes, dried fruit & pickles (torshi)

Also available pre-packed if you’re in a hurry.

Stock the kitchen

Rice, Beans & Pantry

Aisles of basmati and Persian rice, legumes, spices, teas, jams, pickles and the dry goods every Iranian kitchen runs on — in family sizes.

  • Aged basmati & Persian rice in 5–10 kg bags
  • Lentils, chickpeas, split peas & beans
  • Saffron, sumac, advieh, dried limes & spice blends
  • Tea, rosewater, jams, pickles & canned goods
