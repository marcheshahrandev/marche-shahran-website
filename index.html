Our promise

100% Halal — every cut, every day

All of our meat and chicken is certified Halal, cut fresh in-store by our butchers. Tell us how you like it and we’ll prepare it for you — whole, portioned, ground, or marinated for the grill.

  • Fresh beef, lamb & chicken — never pre-frozen on the floor
  • House-marinated kabab, joojeh & barg, ready to cook
  • Custom cuts and bulk orders for gatherings
